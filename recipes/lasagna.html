Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients:-


  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Sausage
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mozarella
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Lasagna

Process:-

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce
  3. Combine Cheese Mixture
  4. Layer And Bake
