Hamburger

Thick or thin, made on the grill or stovetop, this is the best and easiest all-purpose recipe for perfect hamburger patties every time! These juicy, delicious homemade hamburgers are ready in less than 30 minutes and are a must-make for your next cookout.

Ingredients:-

  • Ground Chuck
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Seasonings

Process:-

  1. Use your hands and mix the mixture of groudn beef, milk, egg, cheese,crushed crackers and spices throughly
  2. Press the meat down into even disks
  3. Bake Them
  4. Now grill them
  5. Now put the patties on them and enjoy
