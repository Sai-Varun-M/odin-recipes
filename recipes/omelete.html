Omelete

Making an omelette at home is not difficult. With a few basic steps and a flip of the wrist, you can pull off this recipe in minutes. Fill it with whatever you have on handâ€”it's a great way to use up leftovers.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp onions finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp capsicum finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato finely chopped
  • 1/8 tbsp salt
  • 1 Green chilli Chopped
  • Pinch Of Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Corriender Leaves

Process:-

  1. Beat The Eggs
  2. Pour The Oil
  3. Add The Eggs
  4. Fill The Omelete
  5. Fold And Serve
